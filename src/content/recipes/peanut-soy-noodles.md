# Peanut Soy Noodles

A quick noodle template with a savory peanut-soy sauce.

## Yield
2 servings

## Ingredients

### Sauce
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sugar
- [ ] Pinch of MSG
- [ ] 1 tablespoon peanut butter
- [ ] 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- [ ] 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- [ ] 1/4 cup water
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- [ ] Miso paste, optional

### Aromatics
- [ ] 2 to 3 cloves garlic
- [ ] 3 cm ginger
- [ ] 4 to 5 green onions

### Optional Vegetables
- [ ] Broccoli
- [ ] Bell peppers

### Optional Toppings
- [ ] Roasted peanuts
