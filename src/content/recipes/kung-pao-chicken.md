Stir-fried chicken with peanuts, aromatics, and a glossy sweet-sour sauce.

## Ingredients

### Main
- [ ] 0.5 kg chicken thigh
- [ ] 4 scallions
- [ ] 5 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] 1 knob ginger, minced
- [ ] 1/3 cup peanuts
- [ ] 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- [ ] Dried chilies

### Marinade
- [ ] 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Shaoxing wine
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- [ ] 1 egg white
- [ ] 2 tablespoons cornstarch

### Sauce
- [ ] 2 tablespoons sugar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons Chinese black vinegar, or a balsamic and rice vinegar mix
- [ ] 1 teaspoon light soy sauce
- [ ] 1 teaspoon dark soy sauce
- [ ] 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- [ ] 2 tablespoons water
## Instructions
1. Slice the chicken into bite-sized pieces. Mince the garlic and ginger and slice the scallions.
2. Mix the chicken with the marinade ingredients and let it sit briefly.
3. Stir together the sauce ingredients in a bowl.
4. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat and shallow-fry the chicken for 3 to 5 minutes, until golden. Work in batches if needed.
5. Remove the chicken and pour off most of the oil, leaving about 2 tablespoons.
6. Stir-fry the dried chilies, ginger, scallions, and garlic for 1 to 2 minutes.
7. Add the peanuts and chicken back to the wok and stir-fry for 1 to 2 minutes.
8. Pour in the sauce and cook until glossy and the chicken is evenly coated.
9. Finish with sesame oil and serve.
