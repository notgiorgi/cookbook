A simple beef cutlet mix with onion, garlic, a little celery, breadcrumbs, and egg.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 kg beef
- [ ] 170 g onion
- [ ] 1 garlic clove
- [ ] 5 g celery
- [ ] 30 g breadcrumbs, about 1/3 cup
- [ ] 1 egg
- [ ] 15 g salt, about 2 1/2 teaspoons (1.5%)

## Instructions

1. Finely chop or mince the onion, garlic, and celery.
2. Mix the beef with the onion, garlic, celery, breadcrumbs, egg, and salt.
3. Knead until the mixture is evenly combined and slightly sticky.
4. Shape into cutlets.
5. Rest the mixture or shaped cutlets in the fridge before cooking.
6. Cook in a pan until browned and cooked through.
