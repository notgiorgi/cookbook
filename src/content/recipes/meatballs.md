# Meatballs

A rough meatball formula with blended vegetables for moisture and flavor.

## Ingredients

### Meat Mixture
- [ ] 1 kg ground meat
- [ ] 10 g salt
- [ ] 1 cup breadcrumbs
- [ ] 1/3 cup Parmesan
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 1/4 cup milk
- [ ] 1 cup ricotta
- [ ] 1/2 tablespoon dried oregano
- [ ] 1/2 tablespoon dried basil

### Blended Vegetables
- [ ] 1 onion
- [ ] 1 to 2 small carrots
- [ ] 1 stick celery
- [ ] Parsley or coriander
- [ ] Other vegetables, as desired
- [ ] 1 tablespoon olive oil, to help blending
