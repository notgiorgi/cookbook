# Baked Ziti

A rich baked pasta with a tomato sauce, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella topping.

## Ingredients

### Sauce and Pasta
- 1 package ziti (about 500 g)
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/2 large onion, or 1 regular onion, roughly chopped
- Garlic, minced
- Red pepper flakes
- Dried oregano
- Fresh basil sprigs
- Crushed tomatoes
- Salt, to taste

### Cheese Mixture
- 1 pound low-moisture mozzarella, cubed
- 12 ounces ricotta
- 1.5 ounces Parmesan, shredded, plus more for finishing
- 2 large eggs
- Fresh parsley, minced
- Fresh basil, minced
- Olive oil, for finishing

## Instructions
1. Soak the ziti in warm salted water for 20 to 30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it absorbs most of the water and turns pale.
2. Heat the butter and olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
3. Add the onion and cook until softened and translucent without browning.
4. Add the garlic, red pepper flakes, dried oregano, and basil sprigs, and cook briefly until fragrant.
5. Add the crushed tomatoes, bring to a simmer, and cook until slightly reduced. Season with salt and remove the basil sprigs.
6. Transfer about half of the sauce to a bowl and reserve it for topping.
7. Stir the ricotta, Parmesan, and eggs into the remaining sauce until smooth.
8. Add half of the parsley and basil.
9. Drain the soaked pasta and fold it into the sauce mixture along with half of the mozzarella.
10. Transfer to a deep baking dish.
11. Spoon the reserved sauce over the top and scatter over the remaining mozzarella.
12. Cover with foil and bake at 400°F (200°C) for 45 minutes.
13. Uncover and bake for 15 minutes more, until browned and bubbling. Broil briefly if needed.
14. Finish with more Parmesan, a drizzle of olive oil, and the remaining parsley and basil.
15. Rest for 10 to 15 minutes before serving.
