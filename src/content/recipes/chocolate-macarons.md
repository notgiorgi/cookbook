# Chocolate Macarons

Large chocolate macarons with a simple ganache filling.

## Yield
10 large macarons (20 shells)

## Ingredients

### Shells
- 2 egg whites
- 1/2 cup (100 g) granulated sugar
- 3/4 cup (70 g) almond flour
- 3/4 cup (90 g) powdered sugar
- 1/4 cup (30 g) cocoa powder
- Pinch of salt

### Ganache Filling
- 2 tablespoons heavy cream
- 2 tablespoons chocolate

## Instructions
1. Beat the egg whites until fluffy.
2. Gradually beat in the granulated sugar until glossy with soft peaks.
3. Add the almond flour, powdered sugar, cocoa powder, and salt.
4. Fold until the batter reaches a mousse-like consistency without overmixing.
5. Pipe or spoon about 20 dollops onto a parchment-lined sheet pan.
6. Let rest for 30 minutes to form a skin.
7. Bake at 350°F (180°C) for about 15 minutes, until the shells start to crack.
8. Cool completely before removing from the parchment.
9. Heat the cream and chocolate until just bubbling, let stand 1 minute, then stir until smooth.
10. Cool the ganache until spreadable.
11. Match similar-sized shells, fill, and sandwich together.
12. Rest for 24 hours before serving for the best texture.
