# Focaccia

Slow-fermented focaccia baked in a 22 x 33 cm pan.

## Yield
1 loaf

## Time
24 to 48 hours total

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 500 g bread flour
- 450 g water (90% hydration)
- 10 g salt
- 5 g active dry yeast
- 25 g olive oil

### Pan and Topping
- Olive oil, for greasing and drizzling
- Flaky salt
- Fresh herbs, such as rosemary or thyme
- Garlic, olives, or other toppings

## Instructions
1. Mix the flour and yeast in a large bowl.
2. Add the water and stir until the flour is incorporated.
3. Add the salt and olive oil and mix gently. The dough will be very wet.
4. Rest for 15 minutes.
5. Perform 4 sets of coil folds, 25 minutes apart, covering the dough between sets.
6. Cover tightly and refrigerate for 12 to 48 hours, ideally about 24 hours.
7. Generously oil a 22 x 33 cm pan and transfer the dough gently into it.
8. Drizzle with more oil and proof at room temperature for 4 to 5 hours, until puffy and bubbly.
9. Heat the oven to 450°F (232°C).
10. Dimple the dough with oiled fingers, add toppings, and drizzle again with olive oil.
11. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes, until deeply golden and crisp.
12. Cool in the pan for at least 10 minutes before removing.
