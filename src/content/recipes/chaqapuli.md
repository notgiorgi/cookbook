A Georgian herb-heavy stew traditionally made with meat, tarragon, wine, and tart plums or tkemali.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 kg lamb or veal, cut into medium pieces
- [ ] 4 bunches green onions, chopped
- [ ] 1 bunch fresh garlic, chopped
- [ ] 4 bunches cilantro, chopped
- [ ] 4 bunches parsley, chopped
- [ ] 2 bunches savory greens or peppergrass, chopped
- [ ] 7 to 8 bunches tarragon, stems removed and leaves roughly chopped
- [ ] 500 ml dry white wine
- [ ] Tkemali, to taste

## Instructions

1. Chop the green garlic and green onions.
2. Remove the stems from the tarragon and roughly chop the leaves.
3. Chop the savory greens, parsley, and cilantro.
4. Cut the meat into medium pieces.
5. Mix all the herbs together.
6. Spread a layer of the herb mixture on the bottom of a pot.
7. Arrange a layer of meat on top and add some tkemali.
8. Repeat the layering two more times.
9. Add the white wine, about 300 g per 1 kg of meat.
10. Cover and simmer over low heat for about 90 minutes, until the meat is tender.
