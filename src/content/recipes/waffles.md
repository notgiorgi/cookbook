A simple waffle batter with butter, vanilla, and just a little sugar.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 1/2 cups flour
- [ ] 1 teaspoon baking powder
- [ ] 2 eggs
- [ ] 35 g butter, melted
- [ ] 1 teaspoon salt
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sugar
- [ ] 1 cup milk
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. Mix the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in a bowl.
2. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla.
3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until just combined.
4. Let the batter rest briefly if you want a smoother texture.
5. Cook in a preheated waffle iron until golden.
