A smooth avocado crema with sour cream, lime, garlic, and optional cilantro.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 ripe avocado
- [ ] 120 g sour cream, about 1/2 cup
- [ ] Juice of 1 lime
- [ ] 1 small garlic clove
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] 2 to 4 tablespoons water, as needed
- [ ] Small handful cilantro, optional but recommended

## Instructions

1. Add the avocado, sour cream, lime juice, garlic, salt, and cilantro if using to a blender or food processor.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Add water a little at a time until the crema reaches your preferred consistency.
4. Taste and adjust with more salt or lime juice if needed.
5. Serve immediately or chill briefly before serving.
