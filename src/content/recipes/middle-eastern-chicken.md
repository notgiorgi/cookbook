A street-cart-style chicken and rice plate with spiced rice, crisp salad, and white sauce.

## Notes

- Do not marinate the chicken much longer than 4 hours or it can turn dry and chalky.
- If you are in a rush, you can skip the longer rest and cook the chicken right after coating it.
- For a sauce closer to the modern Halal Guys style, leave out the yogurt, lemon juice, and herbs.

## Ingredients

### Marinade
- [ ] 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- [ ] 3 cloves garlic
- [ ] 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon ground coriander
- [ ] 1/4 cup olive oil
- [ ] 1 1/2 teaspoons kosher salt
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon MSG (optional, but recommended)
- [ ] 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

### Chicken
- [ ] 1 tablespoon olive oil
- [ ] 2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs

### Rice
- [ ] 1 1/2 cups long-grain or basmati rice, rinsed and drained
- [ ] 1 tablespoon butter
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
- [ ] 2 1/2 cups chicken broth
- [ ] Salt and pepper, to taste
- [ ] Pinch MSG (optional)

### White sauce
- [ ] 1/2 cup mayonnaise
- [ ] 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
- [ ] 1 tablespoon sugar
- [ ] 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
- [ ] 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- [ ] 1/4 teaspoon MSG (optional)
- [ ] 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- [ ] 2 tablespoons chopped parsley or dill

### For serving
- [ ] Shredded iceberg lettuce
- [ ] Chopped tomatoes
- [ ] Hot sauce
- [ ] Thinly sliced onion, soaked in warm water 5 to 10 minutes (optional)
- [ ] Pita bread (optional)

## Instructions

### Marinate the chicken

1. Blend the lemon juice, garlic, oregano, coriander, 1/4 cup olive oil, salt, MSG, and black pepper until roughly emulsified.
2. Put the chicken in a bowl and pour over half the marinade, reserving the rest.
3. Massage well to coat, then cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour and up to 4 hours.

### Cook the chicken

4. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Add the chicken in a single layer and cook until browned on the first side, about 5 minutes.
6. Flip and cook until the second side is browned and the chicken reaches at least 74°C / 165°F, about 5 minutes more.
7. Lower the heat if the browned bits start to scorch. Transfer the chicken to a plate and keep the pan with its fat and browned bits.

### Cook the rice

8. Add the butter to the same skillet over medium heat.
9. Stir in the cumin and turmeric for about 30 seconds.
10. Add the rice and stir until glossy, about 1 minute.
11. Add the chicken broth and season lightly with salt, pepper, and MSG.
12. Bring to a boil, reduce to a bare simmer, cover, and cook for 15 minutes.
13. Turn off the heat and let it rest, covered, for 10 more minutes.

### Make the white sauce

14. Whisk together the mayonnaise, yogurt, sugar, vinegar, lemon juice, salt, MSG, black pepper, and parsley or dill until smooth.
15. Taste and adjust. The sauce should be tangy, lightly sweet, and a little salty.

### Finish and serve

16. Chop the cooked chicken into small bite-size pieces.
17. Toss it with the reserved marinade and any juices from the plate.
18. Fluff the rice and transfer it to a serving platter or bowl.
19. Return the chopped chicken and marinade to the skillet over medium-high heat.
20. Cook, stirring constantly, until the marinade reduces and coats the meat, a few minutes.
21. Taste and adjust with salt, pepper, MSG, and lemon juice if needed.
22. Spoon the chicken over the rice and serve with lettuce, tomatoes, white sauce, hot sauce, onion, and pita if using.
