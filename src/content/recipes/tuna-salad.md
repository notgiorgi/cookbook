A creamy tuna salad with lemon, Dijon, a little cream cheese, and herbs.

## Ingredients

- [ ] 2 cans tuna
- [ ] 1 tablespoon olive oil
- [ ] Juice of 1/2 lemon
- [ ] A little lemon zest
- [ ] A few pieces onion or scallion, finely chopped
- [ ] 1/2 garlic clove, finely grated or minced
- [ ] 80 g cream cheese
- [ ] 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- [ ] Salt and pepper, to taste
- [ ] Hot sauce, optional
- [ ] A few coriander leaves

## Instructions

1. Drain the tuna and add it to a bowl.
2. Add the olive oil, lemon juice, lemon zest, onion or scallion, garlic, cream cheese, and Dijon mustard.
3. Mix until combined, keeping it as chunky or smooth as you like.
4. Season with salt, pepper, and hot sauce if using.
5. Fold in the coriander leaves.
6. Serve as a salad, sandwich filling, or spread.
