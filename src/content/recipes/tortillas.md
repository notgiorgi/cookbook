## Instructions
1. Mix everything but the water until the flour has a coarse, sandy texture.
2. Add the water gradually and mix until the dough comes together.
3. Knead on a floured surface until smooth.
4. Cover and rest for 10 minutes.
5. Divide into 16 even pieces, about 35 g each, and roll into balls.
6. Flatten with a rolling pin or tortilla press to about 1/8 inch thick.
7. Cook on a hot cast iron pan until the tortillas puff and both sides are cooked.
8. Keep warm in a tortilla warmer or wrapped in clean towels.
