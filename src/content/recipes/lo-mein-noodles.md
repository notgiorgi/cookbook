## Ingredients

- [ ] Chop up veggies and char them

## Instructions
1. Chop and lightly char the vegetables.
2. Boil the noodles until just cooked.
3. Briefly torch or further char the vegetables and noodles if desired.
4. Cook the aromatics in a hot pan.
5. Add the noodles and vegetables back to the pan.
6. Build the sauce directly in the pan and toss everything together.
